## Part 5: Detailed Doctor Referral Mechanics

### How does a doctor refer 4 patients/month? (Realistic scenario)

**Doctor profile: ADHD psychiatrist with 80 active patients**

**Referral triggers:**
1. **New prescriptions (2/month):** "I just prescribed you Vyvanse. Use FindUrMeds to find it in stock - saves you hours of calling around."
2. **Refill issues (1/month):** Patient mentions pharmacy is out of stock ‚Üí "Try FindUrMeds"
3. **Cost complaints (1/month):** Patient mentions price concerns ‚Üí "FindUrMeds can help you find better prices"

**Monthly flow:**
- 80 patients √ó 5% have issues = 4 referrals/month

**This is VERY achievable for an engaged doctor.**

---

### What does the doctor need to do?

**Option 1: Passive referral (lowest effort)**
- Include FindUrMeds link in prescription emails/patient portal
- Mention verbally when relevant
- **Effort: 30 seconds per patient = 2 minutes/month**

**Option 2: Active referral (medium effort)**
- Keep FindUrMeds cards/flyers in office
- Hand to patients when writing prescriptions
- Text link to patients when they call about shortages
- **Effort: 2-3 minutes per patient = 12 minutes/month**

**Option 3: White-glove referral (high effort - less realistic)**
- Doctor's office staff submits FindUrMeds requests for patients
- **Effort: 15 minutes/month + staff time**

**Most doctors will do Option 1-2, which easily generates 2-4 referrals/month.**
