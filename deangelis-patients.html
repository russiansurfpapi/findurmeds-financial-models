FindUrMeds
FindUrMeds is a medicine finding tool – we help ADHD patients find where their medicine is available and where it is most affordable by calling your local pharmacies (and know how tedious and difficult finding your meds can be :)). We made FindUrMeds to solve our own problem.
How do I use FindUrMeds?
(Optional) Watch how it works — 1 min video from Sasha, fellow ADHDer and founder of FindUrMeds
Choose your dosage, brand, and the quantity of your prescription
Tell us your zip-code, and some details we use to confirm your prescription with the pharmacy (birthday, full name, phone-number, and zip)
We find your medicine and email you / your provider with where you should fill your prescription
Pick up your medicine at the pharmacy we found for you
Use code DEANGELIS for 100% off
Good to know:
• You don't need insurance
• We'll give you a full refund if we can't find your medicine
• We don't supply the medicine — your provider and pharmacy handle that. We just help you find it!
