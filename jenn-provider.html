Provider Guide
FindUrMeds helps your patients find affordable ADHD medication in stock nearby. We call local pharmacies to locate the best options, saving your staff time and ensuring patients can access their prescriptions on time.
How to share FindUrMeds with your patients:
(Optional) Watch how it works — 5 min walkthrough of the provider portal
Visit findurmeds.com/provider/jenn to create a search for your patient
Search for and select their medication
Configure the details (dosage, form, quantity)
Click "Share with Patient" to copy the link
Share the link with your patient via email or text
Patient completes their info and consents to sharing results with you
We call pharmacies to find their medication and email results to both of you
Your patients can use code JENN for 100% off
Good to know:
• Patients don't need insurance to use FindUrMeds
• Full refund if we can't find their medication
• We don't supply medication — you and the pharmacy handle that. We just help patients find it!
• We nudge patients early to help coordinate refills
